What Can DiskGenius Do?
  • Data Recovery
    Recover deleted, lost, or formatted data from hard drives, USB drives, SD cards, RAW drives, RAID, and more, with advanced recovery options.
  • Partition Manager
    Resize, format, create, hide, and wipe partitions. Convert disks between GPT and MBR, repartition HDDs/SSDs, allocate free space, and other features.
  • Backup & Restore
    Safely clone disks or partitions, migrate the Windows OS to a USB/HDD/SSD, and create disk images to protect data or upgrade disks for PCs and servers.
  • Disk Utilities
    Check and repair bad sectors, securely wipe hard drives, convert dynamic disks to basic, monitor HDD/SSD health, manage UEFI BIOS boot entries, etc.
